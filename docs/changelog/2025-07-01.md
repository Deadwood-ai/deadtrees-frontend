# What's New - July 1st, 2025

## 🎯 Easier Dataset Management

**Edit Your Datasets Anytime**: You can now edit your dataset information after uploading! Change authors, update acquisition dates, add platform details, or include additional notes without re-uploading your data.

**Better Dataset Organization**: The dataset table now shows clearer information with improved column names and better sorting, making it easier to find and manage your data.

## 📊 Stay Informed About Your Uploads

**Real-Time Processing Updates**: Get instant notifications when your datasets finish processing or if there are any issues. No more guessing when your data will be ready!

**Clear Progress Tracking**: See exactly where your dataset is in the processing pipeline with visual progress indicators. Know at a glance what's happening with your uploads.

## 🔗 Simplified Publication Process

**Easier ORCID Integration**: When creating publications, you can now paste your full ORCID profile URL instead of just the ID number. The system automatically extracts what it needs and validates the format.

**Better Error Messages**: If something goes wrong with your ORCID information, you'll get clear, helpful error messages explaining exactly what to fix.

## 🎨 Small Improvements That Matter

- Column names are now shorter and clearer (e.g., "Info" instead of "Additional Info")
- Better input field hints to guide you through forms
- Faster loading and more responsive interface
- Reduced duplicate notifications
